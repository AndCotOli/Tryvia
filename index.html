What is this?

Welcome to Tryvia, an online trivia game. Get random questions asked in Trivia games and try to answer them. What will be your max streak?

Why I built it?

I've always liked question games, and what a best way to prove it than building an online clone of Trivia?

How can you play?

Once the game has started, we will answer you a question asked in a Trivia game, and you'll have 20 seconds to select the correct answer. Questions will become harder and harder, how many of them can you answer correctly in a row?
